Fashion Trends/Eras & Music

Welcome to the Fashion Trends/ Eras and Music where you learn about different trends from the 70s to now.

70's

Welcome to a time of disco, bell bottoms, afros etc. For women, the Hippie look of the 1960s carried over to the early 70s. Popular styles included bell bottom pants, frayed jeans, midi skirts, maxi dresses, tie-dye, peasant blouses, and ponchos. . Women in the early 70s who did not go for the Hippie look chose more of a dressy or dressy casual wardrobe.

Mens early 1970s fashion was largely influenced by bright colors and textures. Satin shirts, sometimes with ruffles or lace, were often pared with hip-hugging bell bottoms etc.

80s

Welcome to the 80s.The 1980s were a time of great pop culture including some of the best movies, music, TV shows, and toys of all time. It is the decade most often associated with nostalgia and the style, and memories of the decade, continue to live on. They're also known for Stand by me, Ghostbusters, The Breakfast Club and more.
