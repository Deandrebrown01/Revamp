Fashion Trends/Eras & Music

Welcome to the Fashion Trends/ Eras and Music where you learn about different trends from the 70s to now.

70's

Welcome to a time of disco, bell bottoms, afros etc. For women, the Hippie look of the 1960s carried over to the early 70s. Popular styles included bell bottom pants, frayed jeans, midi skirts, maxi dresses, tie-dye, peasant blouses, and ponchos. . Women in the early 70s who did not go for the Hippie look chose more of a dressy or dressy casual wardrobe.

Mens early 1970s fashion was largely influenced by bright colors and textures. Satin shirts, sometimes with ruffles or lace, were often pared with hip-hugging bell bottoms etc.

80s

Welcome to the 80s.The 1980s were a time of great pop culture including some of the best movies, music, TV shows, and toys of all time. It is the decade most often associated with nostalgia and the style, and memories of the decade, continue to live on. They're also known for Stand by me, Ghostbusters, The Breakfast Club and more.

90s

Welcome to the 90s where the fresh prince of bel air came out, Scream, Buffy, House Party, Daria, Clueless, Poetic Justice and more came out.

Vibrant prints and woven textiles were worn layered with denim, leather, and white cotton in true '90s style. Another popular trend of the nineties was athleisure wear. Tracksuits were practically everywhere, especially in Black urban fashion.

Some women wore baggy jeans others wore skirts and sweaters. In addition, oversized overalls, jordans, jerseys, flannels and more were the trend.

2000s

Welcome to the 2000s, one of my favorite eras. This about the time when shows like Degrassi, The vampire diaries, Twilight, Scary Movie, lil bow wow, Ciara, and More came out.

During this decade people wore y2k apparel, oversized jeans, velour track suits, oversized jerseys and t shirts that would often be paired with timbs. Brim hats would also be worn. Short skirts and denim would be worn as well.

2020-2023

Welcome to 2020-2023 where some of the trends in the other decades have come back. These trends include skate wear, baggy jeans, bell bottoms, von dutch hats etc. There have also been new ones such as nike techs, essentials, beanies, and more.
