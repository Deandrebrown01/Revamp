Here are some fun Facts about the different eras

70s

The popular band "The Beatles" announces they have disbanded. NASA's Apollo 13 Moon Mission returns to Earth successfully after abandoning its mission to the Moon after experiencing oxygen tank problems and an explosion. The first jumbo-jet, the Boeing 747, makes its debut commercial flight from New York to London. Computer Floppy Disks introduced.

80s

>

Early 80s cell phones weighted as much as 20 pounds and were truly huge. Also,80s saw the rise of PMRC, a Tipper Gore-led group responsible for those parental advisory stickers on music albums. Lastly, 80s computers typically had 64 KB of memory

90s

You were not allowed to bring Furbies to work (per the NSA). The theme song to Friends was a one-hit wonder. In addition, Michael Jordan won 6 NBA titles.

2000s

he first iPod was released by Apple on October 23, 2001, revolutionizing the way we listen to music forever. Facebook.com was launched on February 4, 2004, changing the way people communicate. Barack Obama was elected as the nation's first black president of the United States on November 4, 2008.
