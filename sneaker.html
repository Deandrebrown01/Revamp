Jordan XI

These fly sneakers are the black and white Jordan 11's which came out in the 90s and have been released as the retros. The 11's originally were dropped the same year Micheal Jordan announced that he was going to return back to the NBA. Throughout his 1995-96 season Micheal Jordan wore these 11's as a symbol of his return to the NBA. In addition,Its mesh upper provided more durability than the average sneaker, and the patent leather gave the model structure while still providing the necessary lightness. Conveniently, the mix of smooth lines and wavy leather on the simple silhouette made it perfect for dressing up or down.
